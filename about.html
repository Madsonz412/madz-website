About MADZ

A small agency that thinks like an in-house growth team.

Madsonz Ads & Marketing Pvt. Ltd. was built for one reason: to give startups and mid-size brands the strategic thinking of a big agency without the bloat, jargon or six-week sign-off cycles.

Our Story

Started in a co-working space. Still obsessed with proof.

MADZ began in 2020 when three performance marketers got tired of watching startup founders get sold vague "brand awareness" packages with no attached numbers. We started with three clients and a shared spreadsheet of every metric that mattered. Six years later, that obsession with measurable proof is still how every campaign gets built, reported and improved.

Today MADZ is a full-stack digital marketing agency — strategy, creative, media buying, SEO and web — built specifically around the pace and budget realities of startups and growing brands.
